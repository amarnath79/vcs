Our Team

Our Headquarter is in Dublin but we also have an experienced and knowledgeable staff based in India and the Philippines. We recruit internationally from the Philippines, India, Singapore, the Middle East and beyond. We also have experienced nurses and HR professionals as part of our team.

Mission and values

Our mission is to be the leading healthcare recruitment partner in Ireland and beyond. To be acknowledged as long-term, strategic recruitment partners in the Healthcare Sector, due to our dedication, hard-work, professionalism, integrity, commitment, accountability, and care, which are evident in everything we do.

Our Founder

Lalu is the founder and CEO of VCS which was set up in 2011. Since then, Lalu and his team at VCS have helped upto 1000 nurses relocate to Ireland and maximise their potential while assisting many employers in successfully fulfilling their staffing requirements. Lalu Paul has extensive experience in the Healthcare Recruitment in Ireland and is highly involved in the Addiction Services in Ireland.

ABOUT

What we are

A fast-growing employment Agency based in Dublin, Vista Career Solutions (VCS) has been founded by Lalu Paul in 2011, with a vision to assist nurses who aspire to progress their careers in Ireland. With the support of an enthusiastic, ambitious and professionally committed workforce, VCS have partnered with reputable Healthcare Organisations and have been successfully providing the Highest standards of Staffing Solutions while supporting our candidates in maximising their potential.
