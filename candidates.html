Candidates

Supporting you in achieving your career aspirations by providing you with all necessary information and guidance

Our team have a wealth of experience regarding the step by step processes you need to follow, in order to pursue the career of your dreams due to the number of years working in the Healthcare Sector. You will benefit from our on-going support, in-depth knowledge, our established and growing network and personal guidance every step of the way. Our Nurses benefit from support with NMBI registration, RCSI aptitude test, Atypical Visas, Work Permits, Accommodation, PPS, Bank Account and integration support and advice. Furthermore, we extend onboarding services to our overseas candidates by assisting them with Airport Pickup, Accommodation, Relocation, Visa Conversion, Assistance with Social Security services, PPS number, Bank Account etc.
